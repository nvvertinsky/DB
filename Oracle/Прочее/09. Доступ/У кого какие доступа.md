### Привелигии напрямую к объектам
````
select owner, 
       table_name, 
       select_priv, 
       insert_priv, 
       delete_priv, 
       update_priv, 
       references_priv, 
       alter_priv, 
       index_priv 
  from table_privileges
 where grantee = 'NVVERTINSKIY'
 order by owner, table_name;
````

### Все привелигии пользователя
````
SELECT PRIVILEGE
  FROM sys.dba_sys_privs
 WHERE grantee = 'NVVERTINSKIY'
````
